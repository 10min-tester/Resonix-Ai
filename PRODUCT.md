# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want to improve local audio files with AI-guided restoration and mastering decisions on a Windows desktop app. They are listening-focused, compare original and enhanced files, and care about staging, headroom, loudness, tonal balance, and practical export.

## Product Purpose

Resonix AI analyzes source audio and applies a selected listening target while preserving musical quality. Success means the user can trust what changed, avoid clipping or staging loss, compare results fairly, and export a cleaner file without needing to understand every DSP detail.

## Brand Personality

Precise, intelligent, studio-grade. The interface should feel like an AI mastering assistant: calm, technical, readable, and confident.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic neon dashboards, decorative sci-fi panels with no workflow value, landing-page hero treatment, oversized marketing copy, and controls that feel like a toy music visualizer rather than a reliable audio tool.

## Design Principles

- Show the AI decision path before and after processing.
- Keep audio trust signals visible: headroom, true peak, staging, loudness, and A/B comparison.
- Prefer compact professional controls over decorative spectacle.
- Make automation feel inspectable, not mysterious.
- Preserve mobile readability without hiding core controls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high contrast text, clear focus states, reduced-motion safe transitions, and bilingual Korean-first labels with English support. Do not rely on color alone for important warnings or processing states.
